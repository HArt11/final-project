Pharmacies First

Pharmacies are the ideal first point of contact when you're feeling unwell. Pharmacists are trained healthcare professionals who you can speak to without the need for an appointment.

If you are looking for healthcare advice now, you can also visit the NHS 111 website where you can find help on a varitey of conditions, and information on the NHS 111 service.

Clinical services

We provide a range of clinical services, including cholesterol and glucose same day testing, blood pressure checks, contraceptives and inhaler technique checks.

Self Care

If you're feeling under the weather, we can provide help and advice on recovering, while offering a wide range of pharmacy only products.

Prescriptions

We provide a prescrition delivery service for both NHS and private prescriptions. We can arrange to have your prescriptions sent to us to dispense, and provide parcel tracking.

Aesthetics

We provide a range of aesthetic treatments including lip fillers and anti-wrinkle treatments with Botox injections. Safely administered in our clinics after consultation with a fully trained insured pharmacist.

Vaccinations

We can project you and your family from not only seasonal flu, provide Covid, but also travel vaccinations.

Clinical services

We provide a range of clinical services, including cholesterol and glucose same day testing, blood pressure checks, contraceptives and inhaler technique checks.

Self Care

If you're feeling under the weather, we can provide help and advice on recovering, while offering a wide range of pharmacy only products. If we think its something we can't help with we can signpost you to a more appropriate healthcare professional.

Prescriptions

We provide a prescrition delivery service for both NHS and private prescriptions. We can arrange to have your prescriptions sent to us to dispense, and provide parcel tracking.

Aesthetics

We provide a range of aesthetic treatments including lip fillers and anti-wrinkle treatments with Botox injections. Safely administered in our clinics after consultation with a fully trained and insured pharmacist.

Vaccinations

We can project you and your family from not only seasonal flu, provide Covid, but also travel vaccinations.
